Resort 5*, a Deluxe Spa resort in Goa, is spread over 10 acres on the banks of the Baga river and is just a 5 minute drive from the famous Baga beach.This luxury hotel comprises:

  • 77 luxury Deluxe.
  • 5 Royal suites.
  • Two swimming pools
  • An all day dining restaurant (Pickled Mango)
  • Ristorante Italiano and Pizzeria (Mamma Mia!)
  • A Pure Vegetarian Gujarati restaurant (Jalsa)
  • A speciality cafe and lounge bar (Rio CafeBar)
  • And a spa (Zaara), complete with beauty salon and other treatment rooms

In addition, Resort Rio boasts one of Goa's largest conference facilities and can accommodate up to 450 guests theatre style. We also have a dedicated events team for conferences, weddings and banquets.
Whether you are on an all inclusive package or on a bed & breakfast basis, we guarantee you value for money!
